# Dragonfly research
Things I learned:
-Dragonflies have four independent wings
-The body must stay balanced during movement
-There are small weights at the tops of the wings that keep it balanced
-The indevidual cells in the wings are visable and make cool vain patterns
Questions:
-How can I replicate the wing motion with gears instead of muscles?
-How can I hide the electronics?
-How do I desplay it and make it stable in motion?
-What materials are light weight enough?
-What should it be made of?
